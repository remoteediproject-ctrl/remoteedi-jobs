We are looking for a Senior EDI Analyst to join our growing healthcare IT team. This is a fully remote position open to candidates in the United States.

About the role:

You will be responsible for managing EDI integrations with our healthcare trading partners, ensuring accurate and timely exchange of HIPAA transactions.

Responsibilities:

  • Develop and maintain EDI mappings for 837, 835, 834 and 270/271 transactions
  • Onboard new trading partners and payers
  • Troubleshoot transmission failures and data discrepancies
  • Work with internal teams to translate business requirements into EDI specs
  • Document EDI workflows and maintain trading partner profiles

Requirements:

  • 3+ years of EDI experience in a healthcare environment
  • Strong knowledge of X12 HIPAA transaction sets (837P, 837I, 835, 834)
  • Experience with AS2, SFTP and VAN transmission protocols
  • Familiarity with EDI translation software (Gentran, Sterling, or similar)
  • Ability to read and interpret raw EDI files

Nice to have:

  • Experience with HL7 or FHIR
  • Knowledge of clearinghouse relationships (Availity, Change Healthcare)
  • SQL skills for data validation

What we offer:

  • 100% remote, US-based team
  • Competitive salary $90,000 – $120,000 depending on experience
  • Health, dental and vision insurance
  • 401(k) with 4% match
  • Home office stipend
